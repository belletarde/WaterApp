# Privacy Policy for Take Water

**Last updated:** December 2024

## Introduction

Take Water ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

Take Water collects and stores the following information locally on your device:

### Personal Information
- **Weight**: Used to calculate your personalized daily water intake goal
- **Activity Level**: Used to adjust your hydration needs (Low, Moderate, or High)

### Usage Data
- **Water Consumption Records**: Date, time, and volume of water consumed
- **Custom Cup Configurations**: Names and volumes of your custom cups
- **Notification Preferences**: Your reminder schedule and settings

## How We Store Your Data

All data is stored **locally on your device** using:
- **Room Database**: For water intake records and custom cups
- **SharedPreferences**: For app settings and preferences
- **Android Secure Storage**: Protected by Android's built-in security features

### Important Notes:
- ✅ No data is transmitted to external servers
- ✅ No data is shared with third parties
- ✅ No account or login required
- ✅ No internet connection required for app functionality
- ✅ No analytics or tracking services

## How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely within the app to:
- Calculate your personalized daily water intake goal based on weight and activity level
- Track your daily water consumption
- Display statistics and reports (daily, weekly, hourly)
- Send hydration reminders based on your preferences
- Store your custom cup configurations

## Data Sharing

We do **NOT**:
- Share your data with third parties
- Sell your data
- Use your data for advertising
- Transmit your data over the internet
- Collect analytics or usage statistics

## Permissions

Take Water requests the following Android permissions:

### POST_NOTIFICATIONS
- **Purpose**: To send you hydration reminders
- **Usage**: Only used when you enable notifications in settings
- **Control**: Can be disabled in app settings or Android system settings

### SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM
- **Purpose**: To schedule reminders at specific times
- **Usage**: Ensures reminders are delivered at your chosen times
- **Control**: Notifications can be disabled in app settings

### USE_EXACT_ALARM
- **Purpose**: To ensure reminders are delivered on time
- **Usage**: Provides accurate reminder timing
- **Control**: Notifications can be disabled in app settings

## Data Security

Your data is protected by:
- Android's built-in security features
- Local storage (no cloud transmission)
- No external access or sharing
- Device-level encryption (if enabled on your device)

## Data Retention

Your data remains on your device until you:
- Uninstall the app
- Clear app data in Android settings
- Manually delete records within the app

## Data Deletion

You can delete your data by:
1. **Uninstalling the app**: Removes all data permanently
2. **Clearing app data**: Settings > Apps > Take Water > Storage > Clear Data
3. **Factory reset**: Removes all device data including the app

## Children's Privacy

Take Water does not knowingly collect information from children under 13 years of age. The app is designed for general audiences and does not require age verification.

## Medical Disclaimer

Take Water is **not a medical device** and does not provide medical advice. The app provides general hydration tracking and reminders based on common guidelines. 

**Important:**
- Consult healthcare professionals for personalized medical advice
- Water intake needs vary by individual
- The app's calculations are general recommendations
- Do not rely solely on the app for medical decisions
